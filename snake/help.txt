--Modified Snake Game--
Author: Tobey Tan
Date Published: June 2023
Description: A modified version to the classic snake game consisting of additional features such as a timer, varying difficulties, and a local multiplayer option
Instructions: 
- SINGLEPLAYER: WASD or Arrow Keys ==> change snake direction
- MULTIPLAYER:
    - WASD ==> Turns Green Snake 
    - Arrows ==> Turns Red Snake
How to Multiplayer: From the difficulty selection screen, click on the button labeled "1 VS 1" so that it is highlighted. Then click on what of the four difficulties to start the game
